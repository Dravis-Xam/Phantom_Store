Our Development Story

Founded in 2012 by a group of visionary engineers, Phantom Technologies began as a small startup with a bold mission: to revolutionize the way businesses interact with technology. Our first breakthrough came in 2015 with the development of our proprietary AI algorithms that would later become the foundation of our product suite.

By 2018, we had expanded our operations to three continents and secured partnerships with Fortune 500 companies. The pandemic years of 2020-2021 marked a turning point as our remote collaboration tools became essential for businesses worldwide, leading to 300% growth in just 18 months.

Today, Phantom Technologies stands as a leader in enterprise solutions with over 1,200 employees globally and a commitment to sustainable, ethical technology development.

Key Milestones

  • 2012 - Company founded in Silicon Valley
  • 2015 - Launch of first commercial product
  • 2018 - International expansion begins
  • 2020 - Named "Fastest Growing Tech Company" by TechReview
  • 2022 - Reached $1B valuation
  • 2023 - Launched Phantom Green Initiative
